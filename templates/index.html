Overview

Our AI model classifies colon-related diseases based on images, providing early diagnosis for conditions like Normal, Ulcerative Colitis, Polyps, and Esophagitis..

Why It Matters

Early detection of colon diseases like Polyps can prevent colon cancer. Diagnosing Ulcerative Colitis early can help manage the condition, reducing complications.

Disease Classifications

Normal

A healthy colon without any signs of disease. No immediate concern, but regular check-ups are important.

Ulcerative Colitis

A form of inflammatory bowel disease causing ulcers in the colon. Early detection can help in managing symptoms effectively.

Polyps

Abnormal growths in the colon that can lead to cancer if not detected and removed early. Regular screening is vital.

Esophagitis

Inflammation of the esophagus, often due to acid reflux. While not a colon issue, detecting esophageal problems is essential for gastrointestinal health.
